Limited Lifetime Workmanship and Product Warranty

At Unique Cabinetry, we stand by our workmanship and offer a limited warranty on all of our products and services.

Our Limited Lifetime Warranty covers all of our cabinets and finish; however, the hardware and accessories are guaranteed for 1 year. Our warranty does not cover sun damage, scratches, improper cleaning or misuse.

Limitations

  1. While we warrant that our work and products are free of defects and exceeds the industry standards, certain conditions, including but not limited to flooding, fire and excessive wear and tear, are not under our control and is not covered by our Workmanship Guarantee.

  2. In the event that homeowner removes, alters or uses another company to remove or alter our workmanship, in whole or in part, Michigan Woodwork is no longer responsible for any damage or defects.
