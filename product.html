What Sets Us Apart

Below are just a few of the reasons to choose Unique Cabinetry for your next project.
Real Time Design

Our designers offer you the chance to see them design in real time! Using our video sharing platform, you can work with our designers on a whole new level, watching your project take shape right before your eyes.

Michigan Made

Every cabinet is built and designed in-house in Michigan. Unique Cabinetry is in total control of the quality, meaning you can be rest assured that your cabinets will last for years to come.

Eco-Friendly

Being green isn't just a phrase to us. We believe in delivering a product that is sourced responsibly. That means our cabinets have minimal formeldahyde and are sourced from renewable resources.
